Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no cancellation fees.

What counts as a "detail"?

A full exterior wash, hand polish, wheel decontamination, interior vacuum, leather conditioning, and glass treatment. Approximately three hours of studio time.

Is the warranty manufacturer-backed?

The Atelier extended warranty is third-party backed, underwritten by Zurich. Coverage runs alongside any remaining manufacturer warranty.

What's the service radius?

Pickup & drop-off covers 25 miles from our showroom for Signature. Atelier extends to 60 miles plus 4 enclosed-transport trips per year, continental US.
